Work For Warriors Email Templates Overview
Encouraging Closed Position Response
Use when an applicant applies to a position that is no longer open.
Politely informs the applicant that the role has closed while encouraging them to continue exploring opportunities on the Smart Jobs Board.

Interview Invitation Template (Military-Inspired)
Use when inviting a candidate to interview for a position.
Formal and respectful, this template reflects military professionalism and outlines interview details clearly.

Mission-Aligned Follow-Up Template
Use after an applicant expresses interest or applies but before a meeting is scheduled.
Encourages continued engagement with the Smart Jobs Board and offers support aligned with Work For Warriors’ mission.

Initial Contact + Info Request Template
Use after receiving an application or inquiry to begin the intake process.
Requests essential personal and service-related information and invites the applicant to schedule a one-on-one meeting.

Post-Meeting Follow-Up Template (Final Version)
Use after completing a one-on-one meeting with an applicant.
Encourages continued job board engagement and clarifies that the applicant should apply directly to roles of interest. Offers your support if they get stuck.

No-Show Rescheduling Template (Final Version)
Use when an applicant misses a scheduled meeting.
Invites them to reschedule and sets expectations for limited follow-up attempts, while leaving the door open for future support.

Job Offer Coordination Template (Post-Submission Check-In)
Use after submitting an applicant to an employer.
Lets them know you’ve advocated for them, explains that updates may be limited due to confidentiality, and asks them to notify you of any outcomes—positive or negative.

Submission-to-Employer Confirmation Template
Use immediately after submitting an applicant to a job.
Confirms submission, sets expectations about limited communication from the employer, and encourages the applicant to keep you informed.

Rejection Support Template
Use when you learn an applicant was not selected for a role.
Offers encouragement, invites the applicant to share any feedback they received, and reinforces your ongoing support.
